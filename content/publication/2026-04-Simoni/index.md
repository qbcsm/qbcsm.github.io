abstract: We provide an updated scattering library for the simulation of thermal neutron transport in air, including the effects of rotational and vibrational modes in N2 and O2 and neutron magnetic scattering in O2, showing their significance compared to smaller effects related to water humidity. The modeling is based on the Young–Koppel treatment of thermal neutron scattering from diatomic molecules, as well as ab initio simulations of the electronic density of N2 and O2.
